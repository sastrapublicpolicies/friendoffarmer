Registration through SMS:

Farmers can also register by sending an SMS on 51969 or 7738299899. After typing the message send it to 51969 or 7738299899.Farmer will be charged for this SMS. There will however be no charge for subsequent SMSs received from experts & officers.

Kisan Call Centre Registration:

Farmers can call up Kisan Call Center (KCC) through a toll free number 1800-180-1551. Registration of the farmers is done by Kisan Call Centre Agent at the Kisan Call Centre who records personal details of the farmer in the Kisan Knowledge Management System (KKMS).Up to eight options of Crop/Activity can be given by the farmer so that he/she does not receive unnecessary messages for crops/practices he is not interested in. Kisan Call Center services are available from 6.00 am to 10.00 pm on all seven days of the week at each KCC location.
